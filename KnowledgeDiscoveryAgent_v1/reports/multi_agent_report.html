The analysis of Explainable AI (XAI) reveals several key insights regarding its importance and impact:

  • Interpretability: Users require transparent AI systems, especially in high-stakes applications, to understand how decisions are made.
  • Trust and Accountability: Clear explanations enhance trust in AI systems and promote accountability, which is essential for widespread adoption.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Emerging legal standards are pushing organizations to implement XAI techniques to ensure ethical and transparent AI behavior.
